Are You Overpaying Your Heating Bill By 10%?

Learn how Expol Underfloor Insulation can warm your freezing home in winter... and SAVE you money!

It’s pretty well known that while we Australians can bear the heat... our houses just aren’t built for the cold. So what do we do? We turn up the heating... and we pay for it. In fact, according to the ABS, on an average energy bill of $1,600 pa... you’re paying $640 just to heat and cool your home, every year!

But what’s more, did you know your timber floor loses up to 15% of your home’s heat? That means cold feet, chilly rooms and uncomfortable floors. And it means you’re losing more than 10% of your energy bill — money straight out of your pocket, every year.

HEAT YOUR COLD HOME & STOP LOSING MONEY WITH EXPOL UNDERFLOOR INSULATION

Thankfully, there’s an easy solution: Expol Underfloor Insulation.
Made of a specially fire-resistant, non-allergenic polystyrene designed to fit into your timber floor and keep the heat in, it’s the perfect solution to save on your bills and warm your house in winter.
